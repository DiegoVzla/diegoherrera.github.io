About Me

After the pandemic led to the shutdown of the Advance Auto Parts store where I worked as a supervisor, I took the opportunity to switch to a new career and taught myself coding skills such as HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and Backend frameworks. I then joined the 100Devs Agency where I have spent the last year collaborating with other developer teams, working with clients, and delivering their products on time. Additionally, I have contributed to open source projects and pursued my own projects. Thanks to this experience, I have developed expertise in assisting small businesses in establishing an online presence.
