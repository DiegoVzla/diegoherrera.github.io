About Me

I started building websites for local businesses in need of a web presence in 2021 and have provided this service for over a year to supplement my full-time job. I've been in the Auto Parts industry, as well as the Healthcare Industry, and when the pandemic hit, I had a chance to reevaluate my career up to this point. I decided I want to build web applications moving forward. Since making that decision, I have learned HTML, CSS, JavaScript, backend frameworks, and built out my skills as a full-stack software engineer. These skills coupled with years of refining my communication, critical thinking, and other core skills have given me a unique perspective to apply to my work in the tech industry.
